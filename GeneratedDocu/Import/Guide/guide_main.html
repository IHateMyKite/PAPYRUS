Custom devices

There are few minigames at this point. In general, there are 3 types

Default struggle minigame

Will reduce device durability. Crits can occur, and they reduce a greater amount of durability. Following minigames are of this type

Normal struggle

Uses only stamina. Moderate dmg to durability and condition. Uses Pickpocket skill
Add to Durability damage formula BaseDmg + 125% * BaseDmg * (Agility skill) / 100
Damage multiplier Physical Resist - 30% * Condition
Condition multiplier 10%
Stamina usage 75% + 3.5%AV
Magicka usage -40% * Magicka Regen
Health usage -40% * Health Regen
Minimum stamina 25%
Minimum magicka 0%
Minimum health 0%
Exhaustion multiplier 50%

Desperate struggle

Uses stamina and health. Bigger dmg to durability, and small dmg to condition. Durability dmg increases with device reducing durability. Uses Two Handed skill
Durability damage formula BaseDmg + 100% * BaseDmg * (5.0 - (5.0*Relative durability) + (Strength skill) / 100)
Damage multiplier Physical Resist - 20% * Condition
Condition multiplier 50%
Stamina usage 110%
Magicka usage -50%
Health usage 50% + 6%AV
Minimum stamina 20%
Minimum magicka 0%
Minimum health 40%
Exhaustion multiplier 160%

Magicka struggle

Uses stamina and magicka. Moderate dmg to durability and great dmg to condition. Uses Pickpocket skill
Durability damage formula BaseDmg + 100% * BaseDmg * (Magicka skill) / 100
Damage multiplier Magicka Resist - 30% * Condition
Condition multiplier 250%
Stamina usage 65%
Magicka usage 75% + 5%AV
Health usage -80% * Health Regen
Minimum stamina 40%
Minimum magicka 70%
Minimum health 0%
Exhaustion multiplier 120%

Slow struggle

Uses stamina and magicka. Moderate dmg to durability and great dmg to condition. Uses Pickpocket skill
Durability damage formula BaseDmg
Damage multiplier 8% * Physical Resist
Condition multiplier 0% (No condition damage)
Stamina usage -70% * Stamina Regen
Magicka usage -70% * Magicka Regen
Health usage -80% * Health Regen
Minimum stamina 0%
Minimum magicka 0%
Minimum health 0%
Exhaustion multiplier No exhaustion is applied
